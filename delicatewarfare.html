Delicate Warfare

 CAD   3D Printing 

A 3D-printed wearable inspired by biomimicry and costume design from iconic sci-fi films.

Combining 3D photogrammetry, Rhino modelling, and fabrication, I designed and built a spiked breastplate composed of six interlocking 3D-printed parts. Drawing inspiration from sea urchins, the piece is both aggression and adornment—its rigid, spiked exterior acts as a threatening armour, while the smooth interior surface caresses every curve of the body—an exploration of the delicate balance between masculinity and femininity; violence and beauty.
