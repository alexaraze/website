Skyspace

 Computational Design   p5.js   Code Art 

A clock that moves at the speed of the sky. This piece reinterprets James Turrell's Chestnut Hill Skyspace as a digital experience, mapping the passage of time through colour.

The central box represents the sky. Its hue is determined by your device's local time—visit the website at a different time of day, and enjoy a completely different experience. The surrounding frame mimics Turrell's LED ceiling installation, cycling through gradual shifts of colour over five minutes. Just subtle enough to deceive the eye.
